WELCOME TO i-TECH

We, at I-Tech, would like to introduce ourselves as manufacturers of Precision Press Tools, Injection Moulds, Thermoforming Moulds, Jigs & Fixtures, Foaming Jigs, Trimming Dies, and Special Purpose Machines (SPMs).

Established in 2016, I-Tech has grown into a trusted name in the field of tool design and manufacturing. We are committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to our customers’ requirements.

Our state-of-the-art tool room is equipped with both conventional and advanced machining facilities, enabling us to handle complex and high-precision projects with ease. Backed by a team of skilled engineers and technicians, we ensure accuracy, consistency, and excellence at every stage of manufacturing.

At I-Tech, we continually invest in technology, innovation, and skill development to meet evolving industry demands and exceed customer expectations.
