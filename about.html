Innovating Technology for a Smarter Future

Founded in 2016, I-Tech Industries India Pvt. Ltd. has been driven by a passion for innovation and excellence in manufacturing. Guided by our philosophy — “Innovating technology for you...” — we began our journey in the refrigeration industry, delivering high-precision tools and SPMs that meet global quality standards.

With a strong foundation built on technology, skill, and dedication, I-Tech expanded its horizons beyond refrigeration. Today, we proudly serve leading companies in the automobile, electrical, and toy industries, offering comprehensive design, development, and manufacturing solutions.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Avinash Nagarkar and Mr. Mukund Sonawane, the company continues to grow steadily — combining advanced infrastructure with a skilled workforce to meet diverse industrial requirements.

To enhance our manufacturing strength and provide complete end-to-end solutions, we have expanded our operations with two dedicated sister companies:

  • i-Tech Components (est. 2022): Focused on the production of precision plastic components, leveraging advanced moulding technologies to ensure accuracy and durability.
  • i-Tech Pressing (est. 2025): Dedicated to sheet metal part manufacturing, equipped with state-of-the-art press tools and modern machinery for high-performance results.

At I-Tech Industries India Pvt. Ltd., our commitment lies in continuous improvement, customer satisfaction, and sustainable growth. We strive to deliver innovative, high-quality, and cost-efficient manufacturing solutions that empower our clients to excel in a competitive global market.

With a steadfast focus on technological advancement and excellence, we continue to grow as a reliable and trusted partner in precision engineering and modern manufacturing.
